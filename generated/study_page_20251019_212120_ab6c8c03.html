UI/UX Design Study Page

Summary

UI (User Interface) refers to the visual elements of an application or website, including buttons, colors, fonts, icons, and spacing. Good UI is clean, clear, and visually appealing, guiding users intuitively. UX (User Experience) focuses on the overall feeling and ease of use when interacting with a product, ensuring the user's journey is smooth and problem-solving is effortless. Together, UI/UX design plays a critical role in product success, with good design feeling invisible and bad design causing user frustration and abandonment. The importance extends beyond aesthetics to critical systems like medical devices and aviation, where design can impact safety and human life.
